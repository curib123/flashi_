Privacy Policy for Flashi

Effective Date: [02/22/2025]

Welcome to Flashi: AI-Powered Flashcard Generator!

At Flashi, your privacy is our top priority. Our app ensures that your personal data stays secure. You can create, organize, and review flashcards efficiently while using AI-powered assistance for an enhanced learning experience.

1. Information We Collect

  • AI Processing Data: When you use the AI flashcard generator or chatbot assistance, your input (questions, prompts, or flashcard content) is sent to the Mistral API for processing. However, we do not store or retain this data after processing.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or process personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.
  • No Usage Tracking: We do not track how you interact with the app beyond AI processing.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All flashcards, progress, and settings you create are stored locally on your device. We do not access, store, or share this data with third parties.

3. AI and Third-Party Services

Flashi integrates the Mistral API to provide AI-generated flashcards and chatbot assistance. While the API processes user inputs to generate responses, neither Flashi nor Mistral stores these inputs or outputs after processing.

For more details on how Mistral AI handles data, refer to the Mistral Privacy Policy.

4. Permissions

Flashi may request certain permissions for the app to function correctly:

  • Storage Access: To save and retrieve your flashcards and settings locally on your device.
  • Internet Access: Required for communicating with the Mistral API for AI-powered features.

These permissions are used solely to provide the app's functionality and do not grant access to personal files or sensitive data.

5. Ads and Monetization

Flashi integrates advertisements to support its development. We use AdMob as a third-party service to display ads.

How Ads Work

AdMob may collect limited data, such as device information and approximate location, to serve relevant ads. For more details, refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

User Control and Consent

You have control over ad preferences, and rewarded video ads remain optional.

6. Your Responsibilities

As a user of Flashi:

  • You are responsible for safeguarding the data stored on your device.
  • We recommend regularly backing up your flashcards, especially if you intend to uninstall the app or switch devices.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be effective upon posting the updated policy within the app or on our website.
