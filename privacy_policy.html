Privacy Policy for FlashLearn

Effective Date: [01/19/2025]

1. Information We Do Not Collect

FlashLearn is designed to work entirely offline. This means:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or process personal data such as names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • No Usage Data: We do not track how you use the app or collect analytics.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create within FlashLearn, including your flashcards, progress, and settings, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not shared with any third party.

3. Permissions

FlashLearn may request certain permissions for the app to function correctly:

  • Storage Access: To save and retrieve your flashcards and settings locally on your device.

These permissions are used solely to provide the app's functionality and are not used to access or share your personal information.

4. Ads and Monetization

FlashLearn integrates advertisements to support its development and maintenance. We use Google AdMob as a third-party service to display non-personalized ads. Below is a detailed explanation of how ads are implemented:

Purpose of Ads

The inclusion of ads in FlashLearn provides financial support to sustain app development and keep it free for users. This funding allows us to release updates, introduce new features, and enhance the overall user experience.

Types of Ads

To minimize disruption and maintain a user-friendly experience, the following types of ads are used:

  • Banner Ads: Small, static ads displayed at the top or bottom of the interface, designed to be unobtrusive.
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown during natural app transitions, such as between activities or studying.
  • Rewarded Video Ads: Optional ads that users can watch voluntarily in exchange for in-app rewards, such as extra slots or bonuses.

How Ads Work

FlashLearn uses Google AdMob to serve **non-personalized ads**, ensuring that no cookies, unique identifiers, or browsing data are used to tailor ads to individual users. Instead, ads are contextually relevant, based on general information like the app's content or the user’s general location (e.g., country or region). For more details, refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.

Use of Test Ads During Internal and Closed Testing

During the internal and closed testing phases of FlashLearn, we use **test ads** provided by Google AdMob. These test ads are placeholders that simulate real advertisements and are essential for ensuring the following:

  • Functionality Testing: Verifying that ad placements appear as intended without disrupting app performance.
  • Compliance: Ensuring ads are displayed in compliance with app store guidelines and AdMob policies.
  • User Experience Optimization: Checking that ads are non-intrusive and align with the app's user-friendly design.

Test ads prevent unintended clicks or revenue generation during testing, ensuring a fair and accurate deployment when the app is officially released.

User Control and Consent

FlashLearn respects user preferences by offering transparency and control over advertisements:

  • All ads served in FlashLearn during production are non-personalized, ensuring user data is not tracked for ad targeting.
  • Rewarded video ads remain entirely optional, giving users the choice to watch these ads for additional rewards.

5. Third-Party Services

FlashLearn uses Google AdMob to serve non-personalized advertisements within the app. During the testing phase, test ads are used to validate proper ad functionality. FlashLearn does not collect personal information directly. However, Google AdMob may collect basic contextual data, such as general location, to serve ads.

For a detailed understanding of Google’s data handling practices, please refer to their Privacy Policy.

6. Your Responsibilities

As a user of FlashLearn:

  • You are responsible for safeguarding the data stored on your device.
  • We recommend regularly backing up your data, especially if you intend to uninstall the app or switch devices.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective upon posting the updated policy within the app or on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
