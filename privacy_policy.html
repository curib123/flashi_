Privacy Policy for Flashi

Effective Date: [04/16/2025]

1. Information We Collect

  • No Personal Information Collected: Flashi does not require you to sign in or provide any personal information. We do not collect, store, or process names, email addresses, contact details, or any other personal data.
  • AI Input Data: When using features like the flashcard generator, chatbot, or daily trivia, your input (e.g. prompts, questions) is sent to the Mistral API or Trivia API for processing. These inputs are not stored after generating a response.
  • No Usage Tracking: We do not track user behavior or interaction within the app. All actions are processed locally or through one-time API requests without analytics or tracking.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All flashcards, settings, and quiz data are stored locally on your device. You are fully in control of your data and can choose to:

  • Export your flashcards and quiz data to a file for backup or sharing.
  • Import previously saved data into the app to restore your progress.

Scoped Storage (Android 13+): On devices running Android 13 or later, Flashi uses scoped storage. This means the app can only access files you explicitly select and cannot access your broader file system, ensuring greater privacy and data protection.

3. AI and Third-Party Services

Flashi uses the following APIs to provide its core functionality:

  • Mistral API: Used to generate flashcards and chatbot responses based on your input.
  • Trivia API: Used to deliver daily quiz questions with randomized trivia.

These services process your input temporarily to generate a response, but neither Flashi nor these third parties store your content after processing.

Refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Mistral Privacy Policy
  • Open Trivia Database (Trivia API)

4. Permissions

To function properly, Flashi may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To import/export your flashcards or quiz data as files.
  • Internet Access: Required for AI processing via Mistral, daily trivia generation, and ads.
  • Camera or Image Access (Optional): If you want to generate flashcards or questions from scanned documents or images.

Permissions are only used for the purposes described above and are never used to access sensitive or unrelated data.

5. Ads and Monetization

Flashi uses Google AdMob to display ads for monetization purposes.

How Ads Work

AdMob may collect limited information such as device type and approximate location to provide relevant ads. No personal data is collected by Flashi.

User Control

You can control ad preferences through your device settings. Rewarded ads are fully optional and never required to use any core feature of the app.

For more info, view Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Responsibilities

As a user of Flashi:

  • You are responsible for keeping backups of your data using the export feature.
  • We recommend exporting your data before uninstalling or switching devices.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. All updates will be shown inside the app or made available on our official website.
