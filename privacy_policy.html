Privacy Policy for Flashi

Effective Date: [02/14/2025]

Welcome to Flashi!

At Flashi, your privacy is our top priority. Designed to work entirely offline, our app ensures that your personal data stays on your device. You can create, organize, and review flashcards securely and efficiently without worrying about data collection or tracking. We are committed to providing a learning experience that is private, distraction-free, and fully under your control.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Flashi is designed to work entirely offline. This means:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or process personal data such as names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • No Usage Data: We do not track how you use the app or collect analytics.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create within Flashi, including your flashcards, progress, and settings, is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not shared with any third party.

3. Permissions

Flashi may request certain permissions for the app to function correctly:

  • Storage Access: To save and retrieve your flashcards and settings locally on your device.

These permissions are used solely to provide the app's functionality and are not used to access or share your personal information.

4. Ads and Monetization

Flashi integrates advertisements to support its development and maintenance. We use Start.io as a third-party service to display ads. Below is a detailed explanation of how ads are implemented:

Purpose of Ads

The inclusion of ads in FlashLearn provides financial support to sustain app development and keep it free for users. This funding allows us to release updates, introduce new features, and enhance the overall user experience.

Types of Ads

To minimize disruption and maintain a user-friendly experience, the following types of ads are used:

  • Banner Ads: Small, static ads displayed at the top or bottom of the interface, designed to be unobtrusive.
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown during natural app transitions, such as between activities or studying.
  • Rewarded Video Ads: Optional ads that users can watch voluntarily in exchange for in-app rewards, such as extra slots or bonuses.

How Ads Work

Flashi uses Start.io to serve ads. Start.io may collect limited data, such as general device information and location, to provide relevant ads. For more details, refer to Start.io’s Privacy Policy.

User Control and Consent

Flashi respects user preferences by offering transparency and control over advertisements:

  • All ads served in Flashi comply with relevant data protection laws.
  • Rewarded video ads remain entirely optional, giving users the choice to watch these ads for additional rewards.

5. Third-Party Services

Flashi uses Start.io to serve advertisements within the app. Start.io may collect basic contextual data, such as general location, to serve ads.

For a detailed understanding of Start.io’s data handling practices, please refer to their Privacy Policy.

6. Your Responsibilities

As a user of Flashi:

  • You are responsible for safeguarding the data stored on your device.
  • We recommend regularly backing up your data, especially if you intend to uninstall the app or switch devices.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective upon posting the updated policy within the app or on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
